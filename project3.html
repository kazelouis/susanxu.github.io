MealEase Meal Planning App

Role: UX Designer & Product Strategist
Duration: 6 Weeks
Tools: Figma, Canva, Google Surveys

Revolutionizing Meal Planning

MealEase was designed to solve the common problem of meal planning fatigue. Our research showed that:

  • 72% of working adults struggle with daily meal decisions
  • 65% waste food due to poor planning
  • 89% want healthier eating habits

Design Process

User Research Wireframing Prototyping A/B Testing

Core Features

Smart Meal Suggestions

AI-powered recommendations based on dietary preferences and pantry items

Integrated Grocery Lists

Auto-generated shopping lists with smart categorization

Meal Prep Timeline

Visual cooking scheduler with step-by-step guidance

Design Journey

1. Discovery Phase

Conducted user interviews and competitive analysis to identify key pain points

2. Wireframing

Created low-fidelity prototypes focusing on core user flows

3. User Testing

Iterated designs based on feedback from 50+ beta testers
